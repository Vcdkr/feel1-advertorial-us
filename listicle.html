Bulldog Skincare Age Defence Moisturizer

★★★★☆ 7.8 / 10 Best For: Sensitive Skin · Entry-Level Natural

Bulldog is one of the most accessible "natural-leaning" men's skincare brands, and their Age Defence line is well-made for the price. The formula includes green tea, CoQ10, and camelina oil — all legitimate anti-aging ingredients. It absorbs well, has a subtle fresh scent that most men tolerate easily, and won't break the bank.

The limitation is formulation depth. Like most mass-market "natural" products, Bulldog's base is primarily water and glycerin. The active ingredients are present, but at concentrations that deliver modest rather than dramatic results. Good for maintenance; not ideal if you're trying to visibly turn back the clock.

What We Like

  • Very accessible price point (~$14.99)
  • Free from parabens and artificial colors
  • Lightweight, absorbs easily
  • Great entry point for men new to skincare

What Could Be Better

  • Water-based — limited active ingredient penetration
  • Anti-aging results modest at best in our testing
  • No fat-soluble vitamin complex
  • Contains some synthetic stabilizers
Verdict: A solid starter moisturizer for men who haven't used skincare before. But for men over 40 who want real anti-aging results, it won't be enough. Consider it a stepping stone, not a destination.

Jack Black Double Duty Face Moisturizer SPF 20

★★★★☆ 7.5 / 10 Best For: Daily AM Protection · Commuters

Jack Black's Double Duty earns its spot here as the best option for men who want UV protection built into their morning routine. SPF 20 won't win any dermatology awards, but it's better than nothing for daily commuters, and the base formula includes Blue Algae Extract and CoQ10 for some legitimate antioxidant support.

At $38, it's overpriced for what you get. The "double duty" concept makes sense, but the formula is heavily water-based, the active ingredient concentrations are low, and in our testing it provided minimal visible anti-aging results over 60 days. Use it in the morning alongside something more effective at night.

What We Like

  • SPF 20 protection built in
  • Non-greasy, fast-absorbing texture
  • Good as an AM-only product
  • Clean, masculine scent

What Could Be Better

  • Significantly overpriced for its formula
  • Zero visible anti-aging results in our testing
  • Water base limits active delivery
  • Doesn't help with skin texture or fine lines
Verdict: Fine as an SPF step in your routine, but don't expect anti-aging results. At $38 it's expensive for essentially a light water-based sunscreen with marketing language around it. Pair with Feel1 at night for a complete routine.

Brickell Men's Reviving Day Serum

★★★☆☆ 7.2 / 10 Best For: Lightweight Anti-Aging Layer

Brickell is one of the more serious men's natural skincare brands, and their formulations are more thoughtful than most. The Reviving Day Serum contains MATRIXYL 3000 (a peptide complex with genuine research behind it), hyaluronic acid, and several botanical antioxidants. The ingredient list reads well.

The results, unfortunately, didn't fully match the ingredient list. Serums by definition are water-based and lightweight, which means the active ingredients face the same bioavailability limitations as any other water-based product. At $55, we expected more visible results. The fine-line reduction we saw over 60 days was modest. Excellent for men who layer a serum under a heavier moisturizer.

What We Like

  • Thoughtful, research-backed ingredients
  • Lightweight — good for layering
  • MATRIXYL 3000 is a legitimate collagen booster
  • No artificial fragrances

What Could Be Better

  • $55 price point feels steep for modest results
  • Water base limits ingredient delivery
  • Needs a heavier moisturizer to be complete
  • Results plateau after 6–8 weeks in our testing
Verdict: A well-intentioned serum with good ingredients, but the delivery system limits its potential. If you're committed to a serum + moisturizer routine and want the best serum available, this is it. But $55 for secondary results when Feel1 delivers primary results for less is hard to justify.

Anthony Vitamin C Facial Lotion

★★★☆☆ 6.9 / 10 Best For: Brightening · Hyperpigmentation

Anthony's Vitamin C Facial Lotion is well-regarded in the men's grooming space, and for a specific use case — brightening and addressing hyperpigmentation from sun damage — it performs reasonably well. The formula is stable (Vitamin C is notoriously unstable in water-based formulas), which is a real achievement.

However, Vitamin C is water-soluble, which creates the familiar bioavailability problem. Most of the brightening effect comes from surface-level exfoliation rather than deep cellular repair. Not our pick for the core anti-aging goal, but useful as a targeted brightening step if sun spots and uneven tone are specific concerns.

What We Like

  • Stabilized Vitamin C formula
  • Noticeable brightening effect over time
  • Addresses hyperpigmentation well

What Could Be Better

  • Synthetic ingredients in base formula
  • Limited anti-aging results beyond brightening
  • $32 for a narrow use case
  • Can cause sensitivity in some skin types
Verdict: A niche product for a specific problem. If dark spots and uneven skin tone are your primary concern, this is worth considering. For overall anti-aging and skin renewal, it's not the right tool.

Every Man Jack is the pick for men on a tight budget who want to do something for their skin. At $9.99, it delivers better-than-nothing moisture and some SPF protection. The brand avoids parabens and artificial fragrances, which is creditable at this price point.

But it's a reasonable maintenance product if budget is the primary constraint.

  • Good for daily maintenance

What Could Be Better

Ursa Major Essential Face Balm

★★½☆☆ 6.0 / 10 Best For: Extra-Dry Skin · Minimalist Routine

Ursa Major makes genuinely clean products — certified natural, minimal ingredients, no synthetic additives. The plant oils it uses also have a shorter shelf life and can become rancid. For the price, we expected more reliable performance.

What We Like

  • Nice texture and feel

What Could Be Better

  • No meaningful fine-line results in our testing

Frequently Asked Questions

Is beef tallow safe to put on your face?

Yes. Properly refined beef tallow from grass-fed sources is dermatologically safe and has been used for skin care for thousands of years. Feel1 specifically is dermatologically tested and certified. Tallow is non-comedogenic (won't clog pores) and its lipid profile closely matches human sebum, making it well-tolerated even by sensitive skin.

How long until I see results?

Most men notice improved skin hydration and texture within 1–2 weeks. Visible anti-aging results — softened fine lines, improved elasticity — typically become apparent at 4–6 weeks with consistent daily use. More significant changes continue to develop over 90 days as collagen synthesis builds up.

Will it feel greasy?

With proper application (a small amount is enough), Feel1 absorbs within 30–60 seconds and leaves no greasy residue. The jojoba oil in the formula specifically prevents the heavy, occlusive feeling that pure tallow can sometimes create.

Can I use it if I have acne-prone or oily skin?

Yes. Tallow is non-comedogenic, and the jojoba component regulates sebum production, making it suitable for oily and acne-prone skin types. Many of our reviewers with oily skin found that consistent use actually reduced overall oiliness over time as the sebum-regulation benefit accumulated.

Yes. Feel1 offers a full money-back guarantee within 30 days, no questions asked. If you don't see results you're happy with, you can return it for a full refund.
